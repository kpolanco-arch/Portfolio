Project Overview

SolBank is a fictional challenger bank designed to disrupt traditional financial institutions with a seamless, user-friendly digital experience. The brand’s core values— playful, clear, and trustworthy —guided the design process, ensuring that the interface is both engaging and reliable while maintaining a high level of usability.

Brand Identity

SolBank draws inspiration from my Peruvian heritage and the country’s currency, 'Soles,' which originates from theSpanish word for 'sun' and is deeply rooted in Incan history.

The Inca civilization worshiped Inti, the sun god, as their most powerful deity, believing he brought life, prosperity,and strength to their empire. Gold, often called the 'tears of the sun,' was sacred to the Incas, symbolizing divine power and wealth.

These historical influences shape SolBank’s identity—its golden-yellow hues not only reflect the brilliance of the sun but also evoke the opulence of Incan gold artifacts, once used to adorn temples and honor their gods. Through this connection, the brand embodies a legacy of strength, prosperity, and innovation, blending history with modern financial empowerment.

Key Screens

This project includes nine high-fidelity UI designs, ensuring consistency and adaptability across multiple devices:

  • Accounts Overview: A centralized hub displaying all accounts (current, savings, joint), offering quick navigation and financial snapshots.
  • Current Account: A detailed view of everyday transactions, featuring recent spending activity and convenient access to payees.
  • My Spending: A visually engaging dashboard with spending insights, savings goals, and interactive data visualizations to help users make informed financial decisions.
